About This Project

Our Mission & Team

Understanding climate change through Science, Technology, and Society

The Project

Educational Platform

This website serves as a comprehensive educational platform exploring climate change through the multidisciplinary framework of Science, Technology, and Society (STS).

Our project examines how scientific understanding, technological innovation, and societal structures intersect to create both the challenges and solutions related to our changing climate.

Our Purpose

Why We Created This Project

01

Educate & Inform

Provide clear, accurate information about climate change to empower informed decision-making.

02

Apply STS Framework

Demonstrate how Science, Technology, and Society perspectives help understand global challenges.

03

Inspire Action

Motivate individuals and communities to take meaningful steps toward sustainability.

04

Bridge Knowledge Gaps

Connect scientific research with everyday understanding for all audiences.
