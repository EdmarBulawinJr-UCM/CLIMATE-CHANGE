The Crisis

What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in global temperatures and weather patterns that fundamentally alter Earth's ecosystems.

While natural processes have always influenced our climate, human activities—particularly burning fossil fuels—have become the primary drivers of rapid warming since the mid-20th century.
